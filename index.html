Rules:

Catsweeper, much like Minesweeper, is a game where cats are hidden under square tiles. Safe tiles have numbers on them, which show how many cats are on the neighbouring tiles. If there are no cats on the neighbouring tiles, they will open automatically. Your first click is always safe - there is no cat there.

If you're sure that a cat is hiding under a tile, you can mark it with a flag by right clicking or by switching to flag mode using the button in the middle of the game header.

Remember that you're very allergic to cats. If you click on one, you lose the game!
